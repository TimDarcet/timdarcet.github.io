Paris street match tester

Ranks … streets in the whole database by edit distance (lev). The query keeps its prefixes (core(q,false)); each street is keyed both with and without its prefix (core(name) and core(name,false)) and scored by its best key — as the game does, so porte jaune and rue de la porte jaune both reach Rue de la Porte Jaune. Game accepts distance ≤ 1 (green) among reachable streets; 2 is a near-miss (amber). Here we search the entire database, ignoring reachability.
