Paris street match tester

Ranks … streets in the whole database by edit distance (lev), taking the best of the query tried both stripped and unstripped (core(q) and core(q,false)) — as the game does, so porte jaune reaches Rue de la Porte Jaune. Game accepts distance ≤ 1 (green) among reachable streets; 2 is a near-miss (amber). Here we search the entire database, ignoring reachability.
